Welcome to Gram!


Explore sites that will help you step up your gaming needs! Whether it may be guides, walkthroughs, exploits, or plain advices, these websites has you covered! From detailed reviews and news coverage, websites like Steam, IGN, and Kotaku has paved their way to make sure that gamers will be guided and informed on their favorite gaming entertainment.



Steam is the premier digital distribution platform for PC gaming, offering an extensive library of games across all genres for purchase and download. Developed by Valve Corporation, Steam has become a cornerstone of the gaming community, providing not only a vast selection of titles but also a robust set of features including automatic updates, cloud saves, and a vibrant community. Users can read and write reviews, join discussion forums, and even create and share their own content through the Steam Workshop. With regular sales, game recommendations, and a thriving community, Steam is more than just a storefront—it's a dynamic hub where gamers connect, discover, and engage with the latest in the gaming world.

IGN (Imagine Games Network) is a leading website dedicated to all things gaming and entertainment. Renowned for its comprehensive coverage, IGN provides the latest news, in-depth reviews, exclusive previews, and detailed walkthroughs for games across all platforms. Beyond gaming, IGN also covers movies, TV shows, comics, and tech, making it a go-to destination for fans of pop culture. With its expert editorial team, engaging video content, and active community forums, IGN delivers insightful and timely content that keeps gamers and entertainment enthusiasts informed and entertained. Whether you're looking for the latest game releases, industry updates, or in-depth features, IGN has it all.

Kotaku is a premier destination for gaming news, reviews, and commentary, offering a unique perspective on the world of video games and beyond. Known for its engaging and often irreverent style, Kotaku delivers timely updates on the latest game releases, industry trends, and technological advancements. The site also delves into the cultural impact of gaming, exploring how games intersect with society, politics, and everyday life. With a dedicated editorial team that isn't afraid to tackle controversial topics, Kotaku provides a mix of breaking news, thoughtful analysis, and entertaining features. Whether you're a hardcore gamer or a casual player, Kotaku offers a fresh and insightful look at the gaming landscape.
